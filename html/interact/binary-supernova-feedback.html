A brief introduction

This page is an interactive version of some of the plots from my paper Wagg+2024, which investigates the asteroseismic imprints of mass transfer on an accretor star. We used MESA and GYRE to simulate the asteroseismic signals from a $3.0 \, \rm M_\odot$ star that accretes $0.5 \, \rm M_\odot$ from a companion star.

Below you can explore some of our main results interactively and learn about the evolution and asteroseismic signals from our accretor star model. If you'd like to reproduce any of the results or plots from our paper, our inlists, scripts and data are available on GitHub and Zenodo.
