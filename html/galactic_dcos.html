    On this page, I've done my best to explain (without the cryptic jargon and any assumed background knowledge) the motivation behind, methods used and results found by my paper on detecting Galactic LISA sources with LISA. On the right you'll find a table of contents so that you can hop around between topics and as you scroll the progress bar will let you know how far through you are. And before I go on, it's important to point out that this paper was very much a group effort and I'm very thankful to my co-authors: Floor Broekgaarden, Selma de Mink, Neige Frankel, Lieke van Son and Stephen Justham.
