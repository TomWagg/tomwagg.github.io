Imposter

Find the imposter among the citizens!

Instructions

  • One or more players are secretly assigned the role of "Imposter". The rest are "Citizens".
  • Citizens are told a group word that they must use to identify each other.
  • Each player takes it in turns to describe the word without revealing it directly.
  • After all players have given their clues, everyone votes on who they think the Imposter is.
  • If the Imposter is caught, the Citizens win! If not, the Imposter wins!
